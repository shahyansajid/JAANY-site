About

Board

The board is comprised of dedicated individuals that organize and maintain the JAA. The board is split into serveral divisions; officers, honorary officers, directors, committee chairs, and office staff. Elections are held annually, which any member of JAANY is eligible to run for.

See The Board

Founder

Over the span of more than 100 years, we have tried our best to bring a bit of Japan to our members. Mone of this would have been possible if not for our founder.

Read about our Founder

History

Since its founding in 1907, the JAA has existed to serve the Japanese and Japanese American communities. Even after a century of community service and engagement, the organization remains focused and dedicated. It has become an integral part of New York and the greater United States of America.

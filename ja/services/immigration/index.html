Change of Address

Most non-U.S. citizens must report a change of address within 10 days of moving within the United States or its territories. You can change your address online or mail Form AR-11. If you have outstanding or recently approved applications or petitions, you will still need to update your address on pending or recently approved applications or petitions by calling 1-800-375-5283.

For more information, please visit USCIS “Change of Address Information” page.

Change of Name

If you have to change your name due to marriage, divorce, or any other reasons, fill out and mail Form I-90. For more information, please visit USCIS “Application to Replace Permanent Resident Card” page.

Abandoning Legal Permanent Resident Status

For more information, please visit Embassy of the United States Tokyo. Japan “Abandoning Legal Permanent Resident Status” page.

Expatriation Statement

Expatriation tax provisions apply to U.S. citizens who have abandoned legal permanent resident status. For more information, please visit IRS “Form 8854, Initial and Annual Expatriation Statement” page.
